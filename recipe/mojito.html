Mojito

Classic Mojito

Experience the timeless allure of a Classic Mojito, where zesty lime and fresh mint harmonize with white rum, creating a refreshingly vibrant cocktail.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz white rum
  • 1 oz fresh lime juice
  • 2 teaspoons granulated sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 6-8 fresh mint leaves
  • Soda water (club soda)
  • Ice cubes
  • Lime wedges and mint sprigs for garnish

Instructions

  1. Muddle the Mint
    • In a cocktail shaker or a sturdy glass, add the fresh mint leaves and sugar. Use a muddler or the back of a spoon to gently crush the mint leaves, releasing their oils and flavors. Be careful not to over-muddle and tear the leaves.
  2. Add Lime Juice and Rum
    • Squeeze the fresh lime juice into the shaker/glass. Pour in the white rum.
  3. Shake or Stir
    • If using a shaker, fill it with ice cubes and cover. Shake vigorously for about 15 seconds to chill the mixture and combine the flavors. If using a glass, stir well with a spoon.
  4. Strain and Pour
    • If using a shaker, use a strainer to pour the mixture into a tall glass filled with ice cubes. If using a glass, simply pour the mixture into the glass.
  5. Top with Soda Water
    • Fill the glass almost to the top with soda water (club soda). The amount can vary depending on your preference for the level of fizziness.
  6. Garnish
    • Give the cocktail a gentle stir to mix in the soda water. Garnish the drink with a lime wedge and a sprig of fresh mint.
  7. Serve and Enjoy
    • Serve your refreshing Mojito cocktail immediately, and enjoy the delightful combination of zesty lime, fragrant mint, and smooth rum. Stir occasionally while sipping to keep the flavors well-distributed.
  8. Optional Variations
    • Feel free to customize your Mojito by adding a splash of fruit juice (such as strawberry or mango) for extra flavor.
    • For a twist, you can use flavored rums like coconut or pineapple-infused rum.
    • To make it a mocktail (non-alcoholic), simply omit the rum and adjust the sugar and lime juice to taste, then follow the same steps.

Raise your glass and savor the invigorating and timeless taste of the Mojito, a classic cocktail that's perfect for any occasion. Cheers!
